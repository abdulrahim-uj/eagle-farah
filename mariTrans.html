    Your Trusted Partner for Reliable and Efficient Sea Freight Solutions

    At Eagle Farah Cargo Services, we offer world-class maritime transportation solutions, ensuring that your goods are shipped securely and efficiently across the globe. Whether you're transporting bulk goods, containerized shipments, or specialized cargo, we provide flexible sea freight options that meet the unique needs of your business.

    Our Maritime Transportation Services

    Global Shipping Solutions

    We offer international sea freight services from the UAE to any port worldwide, ensuring your cargo reaches its destination smoothly through our global network of carriers and ports.

    Container Shipping

    We specialize in the transportation of containerized cargo, providing both Full Container Load (FCL) and Less than Container Load (LCL) options, depending on your shipping requirements.

    Breakbulk & Project Cargo

    For oversized, heavy, or unconventional cargo, we offer breakbulk and project cargo services, ensuring your goods are transported safely and securely.

    Door-to-Door & Port-to-Port Delivery

    We provide flexible delivery options, including door-to-door and port-to-port services, ensuring a seamless and hassle-free shipping experience.

    Our Maritime Transportation Solutions Include

    Reefer Cargo

    For a wide range of goods, from consumer products to industrial machinery, we offer both FCL and LCL services to suit different shipment sizes.

    Bulk Cargo

    We offer refrigerated containers for temperature-sensitive goods, including perishables and pharmaceuticals, ensuring the integrity of your cargo during transit.

    Customs Clearance:

    Our experts ensure that your maritime shipments pass through customs smoothly and on time, minimizing delays and avoiding unnecessary costs.
